India vs South Africa 1st ODI Live Score

Under-19 Asia Cup 2023 Final, UAE vs Bangladesh Highlights: Bangladesh thrashed UAE by 195 runs at Dubai International Cricket Stadium on Sunday to claim their maiden U19 Asia Cup title. After setting a target of 283 for their opponents, Bangladesh bundled out UAE for 87 runs. Earlier, Ashiqur Rahman Shibli registered a stunning 129-run knock as Bangladesh posted 282 for 8 on the board after getting an invitation to bat first. Chowdhury Md Rizwan (60) and Ariful Islam (50) also gave important contributions with the bat. For UAE, Ayman Ahamed (4 for 52) was the top wicket-taker. (Scorecard)
